# 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by telecom companies. Every customer who leaves results in revenue loss and increases the cost of acquiring new customers.

The goal of this project is to identify the major factors responsible for customer churn and provide business recommendations that can help improve customer retention.

This project demonstrates a complete Data Analytics workflow, including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, business insights, and actionable recommendations.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

The telecom company wants to answer the following question:

> **Why are customers leaving the company, and what can be done to reduce customer churn?**

The objective of this project is to analyze customer data, identify the key reasons behind churn, and recommend practical business solutions.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze the overall customer churn rate.
* Identify customer groups with higher churn.
* Understand the factors affecting customer retention.
* Discover the major drivers of customer churn.
* Provide business recommendations based on data.

# 💡 Key Findings

* Around **26%** of customers have churned.
* Month-to-Month contract customers have the highest churn.
* Customers using Fiber Optic Internet are more likely to leave.
* Electronic Check users show the highest churn.
* Higher Monthly Charges increase customer churn.
* Customers with shorter tenure leave the company more frequently.
* Tech Support significantly improves customer retention.
* Online Security helps reduce churn.
* Senior citizens show a relatively higher churn rate.
* Customers with partners and dependents are generally more loyal.

---

# ✅ Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are suggested:

* Encourage customers to switch to yearly or two-year contracts.
* Improve the customer experience for Fiber Optic users.
* Promote automatic payment methods.
* Bundle Tech Support and Online Security with internet plans.
* Provide loyalty rewards for customers with higher monthly charges.
* Improve onboarding for new customers.
* Develop retention programs for high-risk customer segments.

---

# 📈 Business Impact

Implementing these recommendations can help:

* Reduce customer churn
* Improve customer satisfaction
* Increase customer lifetime value
* Improve long-term revenue
* Reduce customer acquisition costs
